A desktop client for YouTube Music with synced lyrics, deep customization, and a built-in OBS overlay.

Download for Windows All Releases

Synced Lyrics

Lyrics that follow every word in real time. Supports translation into your language and Romaji for Japanese tracks — each with its own font size control. Switch providers on the fly without restarting.

Customization

Make it yours. Choose your theme, tweak font sizes across the interface, and configure the app down to the finest detail — because good software should fit you, not the other way around.

OBS Widget Beta

A built-in browser source for OBS that shows your currently playing track with album art in real time. No extra tools, no setup headaches — just add the source and go live.

Library Management

Your full library at your fingertips. Browse playlists, liked songs, history, and artist or album pages. Search within playlists and see total duration without leaving the app.

Discord Rich Presence

Let your friends know what you're listening to. Kiyoshi updates your Discord status automatically with the current artist, song title, and album cover.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Control playback, toggle lyrics, mute, like tracks and more — all from your keyboard. Space, arrow keys, F, M, L and beyond. Your hands never have to leave the keys.

Multilanguage Support

Available in multiple languages including English, German, Japanese, Korean, French, and Spanish. Auto-detects your system locale.

MP3 / OPUS Export

Save any track directly from the app in MP3 or OPUS format. No third-party tools needed — just pick your format and download.

PIN & Password Lock

Keep your music private. Lock Kiyoshi with a 4 or 6-digit PIN or a full password — useful on shared machines or whenever you just want a bit of privacy.

No ads.

No banners, no upsells, no distractions. Kiyoshi gets out of the way and lets you listen — the way a music app should.
