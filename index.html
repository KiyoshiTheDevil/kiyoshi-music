Full Playback Controls

Shuffle, repeat, autoplay, crossfade (0–12s), queue management, and progress scrubbing.

Library Management

Playlists, liked songs, history, artist and album pages. In-playlist search and total duration at a glance.

Discord Rich Presence

Show what you're listening to in your Discord status — artist, song title, and album cover.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Space, arrow keys, F, M, L and more. Control everything without touching the mouse.

16+ Languages

English, German, Japanese, Korean, French, Spanish, and more. Auto-detects your system locale.

MP3 / OPUS Export

Save your music locally in MP3 or OPUS format directly from the app.

PIN & Password Lock

Protect the app with a 4 or 6-digit PIN or a full password.

Offline Mode

Play cached content without an internet connection. Manage cache size and clear by category.

UI Scaling

Scale the entire interface from 80% to 150%. Adjustable font size from 13px to 19px.
